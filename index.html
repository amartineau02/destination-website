Experience Baxter State Park

Home to 215 miles of hiking trails and over 200,000 acres of wilderness, Baxter State Park is the perfect venture for the adventurous spirit. Whether you want to go whitewater rafting in warm summer or take a relaxing hike in cool autumn, there’s explore and sports for everyone to enjoy all year round.

Summer

Whether you want to go hiking, fishing, or enjoy the simplicities of sightseeing, summer in the North Woods has it all. Baxter State Park holds plenty of opportunities for the adventurous spirit, and there’s plenty of resources to go along with it. Winter

Winter in Maine can create a fun and daring adventure. It may not be bright, sunny and green, but there’s still plenty to do around the park. Snowshoeing and snowmobiling are the most common activities. Be aware: the winter season can be tough. You need to be safe and plan accordingly. Below, we have developed a list of links for you to reference for more information on the park’s winter activities:
